Instructions

  • Every Team owner gets complimentary 100 DreamTeam coins to purchase players for the team.
  • One can buy as many players as he wants using these DreamTeam coins
  • Once the squad is ready, select the playing XI. You'll get to change your squad every week
  • There must be minimum 5 bowlers, one wicket keeper and at most 4 foreign players in the playing XI
  • Now, you are ready to enter the league, points will be allocated to each player on their performance in the IPL and thus your score would be the sum of the points of the playing XI
  • Points will also be added to the players who were bought but not selected in the playing eleven, obviously the ratio would be quite less rather half
  • For eg: If Virat Kohli is assigned 100 points for his performance, you'll get 100 points if you have him in playing eleven, or 50 if bought but not in the eleven, or 0 if not bought

May the wisest team owner win!
